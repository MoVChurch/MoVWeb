O Merciful Mother of Victory, we acknowledge thee as our most powerful refuge in our times of trouble and we fly unto thee for your patronage. We trust in your great power of influence on Jesus, your beloved Son. As you saved the host of the wedding feast at Cana from the impending shame, we seek your intercession with child-like confidence in all our afflictions. Help us O Mother of Victory to be relieved from our present sorrows (offer your special need in silence here).

O Holy Mother, you who became victorious by crushing the head of the devilish serpent, come to my aid at this dire need of mine and obtain for me from your divine son Jesus Christ all the grace necessary to face my present distress.

Amen.

We humbly ask for your grace to surround (child's name) in this sacred moment, filling him/her with a profound sense of your presence. Help him/her to surrender to your will and trust in your plan for his/her life. May this communion be the beginning of a journey filled with purpose and mission, where (child's name) can serve you with a heart full of love and devotion. We also surrender the day allotted for the solemn communion into your loving hands. In your holy name, we pray. Amen.
