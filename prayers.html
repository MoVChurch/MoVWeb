Dear Lord Jesus, we come before you in awe and gratitude for the immeasurable love you have shown us through the gift of the Holy Eucharist. As (child's name) prepares to approach your holy table for the first time (solemnly), we pray for a deepening of his/her faith and understanding. May he/she continue to grow in the knowledge and love of you. Grant wisdom and strength to us, that we may guide (child's name) in his/her spiritual journey with love and devotion. We humbly ask for your grace to surround (child's name) in this sacred moment, filling him/her with a profound sense of your presence. Help him/her to surrender to your will and trust in your plan for his/her life. May this communion be the beginning of a journey filled with purpose and mission, where (child's name) can serve you with a heart full of love and devotion. We also surrender the day allotted for the solemn communion into your loving hands. In your holy name, we pray. Amen.

Mother of Victory, Pray for us.
